FREELANCE DESIGN CONTRACT

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between XYZ Corporation ("Client") and Freelancer ("Designer").

1. SCOPE OF WORK
Designer shall provide graphic design services as requested by Client. Designer shall make unlimited revisions until the work is satisfactory in Client's sole judgment and discretion. All modifications shall be made as necessary without limitation.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Client agrees to pay Designer upon completion of services. Payment shall be made within ninety (90) days following receipt of invoice, subject to Client's approval of all deliverables. All payments are in arrears and remittance is made quarterly.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
This is a work-for-hire arrangement. All proprietary rights, including intellectual property rights, shall vest in Client immediately upon execution of this Agreement. Designer agrees to waive all moral rights and complete assignment of intellectual property to Client with perpetual exclusive rights for derivative works.

4. INDEMNIFICATION
Designer agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Client from any and all claims, losses, damages, and expenses without limitation. Designer shall be liable for all consequences arising from Designer's work and shall assume all risk. Designer agrees to waive any claims against Client.

5. TERMINATION
Client may terminate this Agreement at will without cause and with no obligation to make further payment. Upon termination notice, Designer shall cease all work immediately with no further payment owed.

6. NON-COMPETE
Designer agrees not to work with any competing clients for a period of two (2) years following completion of services.

7. LIQUIDATED DAMAGES
Designer shall pay liquidated damages of $1,000 per day for any late delivery or penalty for delays.
