FREELANCE DESIGN CONTRACT

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between ABC Studios ("Client") and Jane Designer ("Designer").

1. SCOPE OF WORK
Designer shall provide graphic design services as outlined in Exhibit A. The project includes two (2) rounds of revisions. Any additional revisions beyond the included rounds will be billed at the Designer's hourly rate.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Client agrees to pay 50% upfront deposit before work begins. Final payment of remaining 50% is due within fifteen (15) days of project completion. Designer retains the right to withhold final files until full payment is received.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Upon full payment, Designer grants Client a limited non-exclusive license to use the work for the agreed purpose. Designer retains ownership of all work files, preliminary designs, and the right to display the work in their portfolio. Client may not create derivative works without Designer's written permission.

4. INDEMNIFICATION
Each party agrees to indemnify the other for claims arising solely from their own negligence or breach of contract, limited to the total project value.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with seven (7) days written notice. Upon termination, Client agrees to pay Designer for all work completed to date on a pro-rata basis, plus a kill fee equal to 25% of remaining project value.

6. TIMELINE
Designer shall deliver final work within twenty-one (21) days of receiving upfront deposit and all required materials from Client. Timeline may be extended by mutual agreement.

7. REVISIONS
Project includes two (2) rounds of revisions. Revisions must be requested within five (5) business days of receiving deliverables. Additional revisions will be billed separately.
